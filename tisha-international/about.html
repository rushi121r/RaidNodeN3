Built on Trust, Quality, and Long-Term Business Commitments

Tisha International was established in 2002 with a focused mission: to provide business-ready USA imported refurbished laptops and desktops to the Indian market. From Ahmedabad, we have built long-standing relationships with resellers, IT companies, and wholesale partners who require dependable quality in bulk.

Our process emphasizes responsible sourcing, technical inspection, grade transparency, and stable supply planning. Over two decades of experience in refurbished electronics has enabled us to become a trusted procurement partner for organizations that prioritize performance, pricing, and continuity.

We believe in durable partnerships, not one-time transactions. Every order is supported with professional communication, practical timelines, and product consistency aligned with business use cases.
